A dedicated environmental monitoring device for indoor air and soil conditions.

## Hardware

- ESP32-C3 Supermini
- SPS30 particulate sensor
- ENS160 (eCO₂ + TVOC)
- 3× soil moisture sensors
- 0.96” OLED
- Ventilated resin enclosure

## Features

- Real-time air quality tracking
- Environmental + soil monitoring
- ESP-NOW communication to Home Dock
